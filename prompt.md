# VIDEO GENERATION TASK: "BLOOD, POTASSIUM, AND TREACHERY"

## 🎥 CINEMATOGRAPHIC SPECIFICATIONS
- **Visual Style:** Neo-noir Tropical Surrealism. 
- **Film Stock:** 35mm, high grain, deep shadows, anamorphic lens flares.
- **Lighting:** High-contrast "Jaundice" lighting. Flickering yellow neon, harsh sunset orange, and deep bruised purples.
- **Atmosphere:** Humid, thick with steam and "fructose dust." Heavy tropical rain starts mid-scene.

## 📍 SETTING: THE "YELLOW DEATH" SMOOTHIE BAR
- A decaying roadside shack. 
- A massive, rusted industrial blender sits center stage like a metallic altar.
- Background: A giant, sad inflatable banana on the roof slowly deflating throughout the scene.

## 🎭 CHARACTER PROFILES
- **MARIA:** Eyes the color of "overripe plantains." Sharp, cynical features. Wearing a linen apron stained with fruit enzymes.
- **HUAN:** Sun-scorched skin, trembling hands, clutching a bruised bunch of Cavendish bananas like a dying bird.

## 🎞️ SCENE CHRONOLOGY & BEATS
1. **The Standoff:** Mid-shot. Maria stands by the blender. Huan confronts her, shaking the bananas. The air is thick with tension.
2. **The Reveal:** Extreme close-up. Maria’s hand reaches into her apron and pulls out a **single, glowing, deep-crimson cherry**. It is the only non-yellow object in the frame, appearing alien and vibrant.
3. **The Climax:** Maria throws the bananas, the cherry, and a stack of crumpled paper bills into the blender.
4. **The Grind:** Slow-motion macro shot of the blender blades pulverizing the "Symbol of the Boss" (banana), the "Truth" (cherry), and the "Capital" (money) into a thick, uniform, grayish-pink sludge.
5. **The Communion:** Maria and Huan drink from plastic cups. Their faces are illuminated by a flickering neon sign.

## 🧪 SYMBOLIC TEXTURES & PHYSICS
- **Liquid Physics:** The smoothie should look viscous, heavy, and unappetizingly "homogenized."
- **Organic Decay:** Close-ups should show "bruised" textures of fruit skins and the "starchiness" of the air.
- **VFX:** A lightning strike outside reflections in the blender's chrome surface at the moment the cherry is revealed.
